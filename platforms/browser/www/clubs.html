About Club

Apart from regular curriculum, students should get ample opportunity to explore different possibilities of lifelong learning. They need to continuously improve their skill sets, learn time management and work in collaboration with others. To provide such opportunities, ABESIT has formed various Techno Cultural Clubs under the patronage of different departments with a faculty coordinator attached to each club.These clubs are ABESIT clubs and not any department specific club. To facilitate students in this endeavor, eight clubs have been formed (Four Technical and Four Cultural) as follows:
